# Pain Recorder

Track and understand your pain patterns with ease.

## About the App

Pain Recorder is a simple, privacy-focused app that helps you log and monitor your pain levels over time. Whether you're managing chronic pain or recovering from an injury, keeping a detailed record can help you and your healthcare provider make better decisions.

## Features

- **Quick Logging** - Record pain levels in seconds with an intuitive interface
- **Body Map** - Tap to indicate exactly where it hurts
- **Intensity Scale** - Rate pain from 1-10 with visual feedback
- **Notes & Triggers** - Add context about activities, weather, or other factors
- **History & Trends** - View your pain patterns over days, weeks, or months
- **Export Data** - Share reports with your doctor in PDF or CSV format
