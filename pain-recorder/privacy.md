# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Pain Recorder ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- Pain logs and entries you create
- Notes and descriptions you add
- Account information (if you create an account)

### Automatically Collected Information

- Device type and operating system
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports and diagnostics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the Pain Recorder app
- Generate insights and visualizations of your pain patterns
- Improve and optimize the app experience
- Respond to your requests and support inquiries

## Data Storage

Your pain data is stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to enable cloud backup, your data will be encrypted and stored securely on our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Export your data
- Delete your data
- Opt out of analytics

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@example.com.
