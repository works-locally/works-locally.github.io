# Privacy Policy for Pain Recorder

**Last updated:** 26 January 2026

---

## Overview

Pain Recorder is a completely offline application designed to help users record and visualize pain levels in different parts of the body.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This app is built to work **without collecting, storing, or sharing any personal data**.

---

## Information We Collect

**Pain Recorder does not collect any personal information or user data.**

Specifically:

- No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or identifiers are collected
- No location data is collected
- No device identifiers are collected
- No analytics, tracking, or usage data is collected
- No health data is transmitted anywhere

All pain records entered by the user are stored **locally on the device only**.

---

## How Your Data Is Used

- Any data you enter (such as pain level, body part, or date) is stored only on your device
- The data is used solely to display charts and history within the app
- The app works entirely offline

We do **not**:

- Upload data to servers
- Sync data to the cloud
- Share data with third parties
- Sell or monetize data in any way

---

## Children’s Privacy

Pain Recorder is suitable for children and families.

- The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children
- No accounts, logins, or profiles are required
- No communication features are included
- No advertising is shown
- No behavioral tracking is used

Because the app does not collect any data at all, it complies with children’s privacy protection requirements, including COPPA principles.

---

## Advertising

Pain Recorder contains **no advertisements**.

---

## Third-Party Services

Pain Recorder does not use any third-party services such as:

- Analytics SDKs
- Advertising SDKs
- Social media SDKs
- Crash reporting tools

---

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

- We do not have access to your data
- We cannot view, copy, or transmit your data
- Data security depends on your device’s own security settings

---

## Data Deletion

- You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app
- There is no remote data to delete, as nothing is stored externally

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the app’s functionality changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
Any updates will be reflected with a revised **Last updated** date.
